Our Weekly Music Pick

This page updates every Sunday at 12:00 pm (UTC-5)


2/22/26

This week's pick is "For the Departed" by Shayfer James. Released in November of 2011 (15 years old!!), on the album "Counterfeit Arcade", this song is a perfect way to celebrate the upcoming tour.

That's right! Shayfer James will be on tour performing a continuous live set of the "Counterfeit Arcade" and "Summoning" albums in the United States of America.

If you enjoy soundtracks that sound straight out of a theatric musical, you'll enjoy "For the Departed" and the whole "Counterfeit Arcade" album!
